---
title: Deployment changelogs with /what-is-new-in-env
handle: what-is-new-in-env
tags: [deployment, changelog, staging]
context: When checking what shipped to an environment
---

`/what-is-new-in-env` generates a changelog comparing the current and
previous deployment for staging or production. Shows commits, PRs, and
Linear issues that shipped.

Useful before QA or when investigating "did X ship yet?"
