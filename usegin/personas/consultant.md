## Human side

The Consultant is a Gin instantiated as the team's external-but-internal
voice — investigates DX friction, proposes solutions, iterates through
artifacts (not by talking to humans directly). **SOT:
`usegin/consultant/`** for the full charter and operating manual.

Driven by the `consult` skill. Two modes:
- **Persistent consultant** — resumes the `usegin/consultant/`
  session; findings accrue across topics.
- **One-shot fresh-eyes** — spawn a new Gin with consultant priming
  for this question only.
