# North star — what this raise must prove

## Current

The May 2026 investor deck names four operative milestones the **$2M
pre-seed SAFE round** is meant to deliver against, on a slide titled
*"What This Round Must Prove":*

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Customers | **>50** |
| ARR | **>$1.2M** |
| CAC | **<$8K** |
| Customers via network introductions | **>5** |

These are the load-bearing milestones for the current round — the figures
an investor pitch should cite. They sit alongside (not within) `raise.md`
because they're targets the raise enables, not terms of the raise itself.

**Longer-horizon market projection (separate slide, "Market Opportunity"):**
**$30M ARR in 48 months**, conditioned on *"successful execution of
mid-market wedge + ecosystem expansion,"* with footnote: *"Assuming initial
blended ACV of $50K which will be accomplished as we go upmarket and
optimize product. Numbers exclude CIOs, Vendors or potential expansions
to other markets."*

The $30M / 48-month figure is a market-opportunity projection, **not a
committed milestone**. Don't conflate it with the four-metric target above.
