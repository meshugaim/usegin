# ICP — Ideal Customer Profile and Wedge

## Current

**Primary buyer:** Partner or Head of Professional Services / Head of Delivery at a **mid-sized IT consultancy / systems integrator** ($20M–$200M revenue) running multiple concurrent ERP / complex IT implementations.

**Wedge / entry motion:** **automated weekly status reports** — a low-friction recurring process every consultancy already does manually. Trust is built in "calm periods"; escalation triage is the expansion play, not the entry.

**Why mid-size specifically:** short decision chain (the partner who suffers is the partner who can approve), avoids enterprise procurement / security-review blockers, has enough escalation surface to feel the pain repeatedly.

**Avoid:** large enterprises (procurement blocker), pure SaaS professional services (simpler projects), front-line PMs (wrong level).

**Secondary (inbound-only):** GMs / ops leaders at construction/infrastructure firms managing multi-subcontractor projects. Persistent inbound; not actively pursued (no founder-market fit per Guy).
