# Pricing

## Current

**Per-firm flat-rate bundles, billed monthly or annually. No per-seat pricing.** Three published tiers (as of Apr 2026):

| Tier | Monthly | Projects | Effective per-project | Annual |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | $500 | 5 | $100/mo | $6,000 |
| Growth | $1,800 | 20 | $90/mo | $21,600 |
| Standard | $4,000 | 50 | $80/mo | $48,000 |

Standard is intentionally **sub-$50k** to stay under enterprise procurement thresholds and enable credit-card buys. Self-service upgrade within the UI is on the roadmap.

**Investor-pitch ACV:** $20k–$70k range cited; **blended ACV target $50k** in the May 2026 deck.

## ROI / value math

**Stated 10x ROI floor** comes from this arithmetic:

- Cost per escalation: **~$100k** (unbillable partner time + make-goods + reputation)
- Escalation rate: ~50% of complex IT projects
- Annual loss for a mid-size firm ($50M revenue, $5M profit): **$1M–$2M** in profit margin
- AskEffi annual cost (Standard): **$48k**
- Result: ROI ≥ 10x even at low adoption

Investor-pitch version uses $20k/yr against a $500k–$1M loss for the same firm size, yielding 25x–50x; "10x" is used as the conservative floor.
