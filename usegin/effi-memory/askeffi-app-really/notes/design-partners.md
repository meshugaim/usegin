Possibilities: DreamLabs (UX, Tier-2 in Jan 2026 internal meeting) or another company in the offline design-partner inventory Google Doc not indexed in the project. **Treat the numbering as Guy-internal bookkeeping that doesn't fully map to indexed evidence.**

**C-2 — Epsilon's parent vertical described inconsistently.**
- 2026-04-29 Puneet meeting: *"part of a big media buying agency"* — fathom:99ae2822.
- 2026-05-05 Abdul Ly: *"a RevOps team that does Salesforce projects, part of a very big edtech company"* — fathom:588c6fdb.
- 2026-04-02 SharePoint meeting notes: Publicis Group parent.
- May 2026 deck: *"Rev ops team in large Ad-tech company"* — attachment:e7da01c3.

The Publicis link makes "media/advertising" the precise reading. *"EdTech"* is an outlier — likely transcription error or Guy describing a different entity in passing. **Treat parent vertical as media/advertising; flag the edtech wording when comparing to Effi outputs.**

**C-3 — Curtis Partition PoC timeline wording.** The Curtis PoC email lists *"January 2025"* — almost certainly a transcription error; should read **2026** (AskEffi was incorporated late 2025 and all surrounding meetings are Dec 2025 – Feb 2026). Treat all Curtis dates as 2026.

1. *"IT Consultancy looking to use product with their customers"* — matches Mkenga (active).

The deck does not mention Perform Media (signed never used), Curtis Partition (withdrew), AlignOrg (dormant), Urban Insights, Alcenta, or Critical Loop by name or description.

**C-5 — "First field deployment" timing.** Internal investor update (~Apr 2026): *"An early design partner working on a complex IT implementation has started piloting Effi on a live client project — our first real field deployment"* — gmail:8661c76b. This aligns with Mkenga 2026-04-08.
