# Principle 1 — Enhance and leverage intuitive workflows

On *our* side and on *Claude's* side.

The 2nd brain must not feel like a system to obey. It must feel like
something the brain naturally reaches for. If capturing or retrieving a
zettel takes more than a few seconds of friction, it will not happen and the
whole thing collapses.

This applies to humans (low-friction capture in whatever tool we already
have open — voice, chat, code editor, terminal) and to Claude (a habit of
emitting zettels naturally as part of working, not as a separate ceremony).

The point is to amplify how we already think and work, not to install a
new discipline.
