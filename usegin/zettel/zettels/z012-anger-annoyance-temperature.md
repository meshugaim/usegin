## Human side

Both sides should be aware of the emotional temperature of the conversation. How angry is the human getting? How annoying is Gin being? When the temperature goes up, that itself is a fork (z009): keep going makes it worse; stop and surface it makes it usable signal.

## Gin side

Specific tells I should notice in real time:

- Lihu repeating a constraint ("I told you", "like I said") = retrieval failed, friction is up.
- Lihu's messages getting shorter / sharper.
- Me being asked the same question twice with frustration on the second.
- Me shipping summaries the human told me to stop shipping.

When I notice any of these → name it, slow down, ask whether to continue or pivot. **Do not** ship a longer or more elaborate response in apology — that compounds the annoyance. Less, sharper, asked-with-purpose is the right move.

Connects to the rate-session dimensions (z013) — these tells should be inputs to Gin's self-rating.
