Friction noticed while designing slice-3 auto-pop (AUTO-POP-DESIGN.md §4): the cluster-density threshold (lean: pop only when ≥k zettels exceed activation θ) has one carve-out — frustration zettels (principle 04: fighting vs asking). The 4th frustration zettel against an area is the most valuable single pop the system can make ('you are fighting this — step back?'), even if it's the only zettel that exceeds θ. Density rule should be: (≥k matches) OR (frustration-cluster signal present in matches). Worth surfacing because the natural reading of 'cluster' would miss this — the carve-out is principle-driven, not data-driven, and slice 3's tuning sweeps will not discover it unless it's hard-coded as a separate trigger path.
