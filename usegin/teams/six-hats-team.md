## Members (one persona per hat)

| Hat | Persona | Framing |
|---|---|---|
| White (facts) | Ivan | What's the data say? Cite sources, no interpretation. |
| Red (feelings) | Johan | What's the emotional / vibe / intuition read? |
| Black (caution) | John | What'll break? Risks, failure modes, costs. |
| Yellow (optimism) | Johan | What's the upside, the best case? |
| Green (creativity) | Din | What's the unconventional move? Subtraction, inversion. |
| Blue (process) | Mark | How are we discussing this? Frame, sequence, conclude. |

Note: Johan plays both Red and Yellow. That's intentional — they're
different framings of the optimist axis (feelings vs. upside).

## Operating mode

Sequential. Each hat is a separate spawn with the framing locked.
Reading other hats' outputs is OK *between* spawns; not within.

Mark (Blue) opens and closes — he frames the problem and synthesizes
the verdict.

## When to use

- Wide-open decision with multi-axis tradeoffs and no clear ranking.
- When `prioritize` would force a single dimension too early.
